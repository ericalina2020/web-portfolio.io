PARTICLEUS

This mobile application aims to provide basic knowledge of elementary particles to all learners around the world (especially kids). It contains simple games and graphics which acquaint one with some of the elementary particles by playing the memory and matching game, and quark combinations through naming baryons and mesons. Also, familiarize yourself with electron-positron annihilation.

LIKNAYAN

Liknayan ("Liknayan" - a native Tagalog translation of "Physics", from the root words: "likas" + "hanayan") is an educational app for Physics learning. This app aims to provide a mobile learning experience for Grade 10 learners in Physics through digital copies of Self-Learning Modules (SLMs). The SLMs were derived from the Department of Education Region V - Division of Camarines Sur. Liknayan includes a Physics AI Quiz (with LeoWiz, an AI Quizzerbot) which tests the user's knowledge of the subject. In addition, this app provides an AI note that enables users to write notes either through speech recognition or manual typing.
